Origin

Origin

From the primordial instincts,
made by a genetic trial.

Worshipping metaphysic beings,
masked by false gods.

Directing gazes towards the cosmos
searching for protection,
in the grip of a lie that could destroy a certitude,
in this poor, damned existence.

Modeling different cultures,
dominated by fear of death,
left to live the existence
believing to be part of a divine project,
the unaware cavy of an already planned destiny,
living unconsciously their own sufferance.

Domesticated over time,
living as androids,
as slaves of social conditions imposed on themselves,
made to satisfy their needs,
covering up the truth
of our origins.

Ultimatum

Ultimatum

Rips, encroach,destroy,
this is your nature,
impossible to co-exist,
desire of power,
parasite.

Fragile, disconnected, cross,
fueled by hate,
condemned to suffer,
perverse nature,
downfall.

Your time is over , can you feel?
the balance will return stable,
the process has begun, can you see?
elevation path.

A new aeon has come, can you feel?
the laments of this fertile land,
voices of suffering in your mind,
suffocating shouts.

A new aeon has come, can you feel?
the weight of your awareness,
your light has gone, leave your breath.

Embrace your end, accept the change,
the ascent is now, a new era,
it’s time to restore the cosmic law.

Can you see it? feel your end,
your mandate is over,
open your eyes.

Unnatural, irregular, adverse,
asleep for eons,
deleting your nature.

Living the illusion through your end,
the spiritual connection has begun.

Life after life, time after time,
reborn through the earth,
spiritual awaking making the path,
the new rise of Gaia!

Contact with the Entity I

Contact with the Entity I

- Illuminate us.
What happens when we die?
- Death is a human construct.
It does not exist.
You will experience and have experienced, every instance of so called life.
You. Me. Him.
We are Instances of the same life.
Separated by what you call death.
- So let me get this straight.
There's no death and we all experience each other lives?
- In essence, yes
- So, how was the universe created and why is it so perfectly made for us?
- There are an infinite number of universes, each with different physical properties.
Virtually all do not support life such as you know it.
We exist in a universe that does support so called life.

Contact with the Entity II

Contact with the Entity II

Alone, far from your fellows
looking for a touch,
passing through a divine path
reconciling with it.

Like a drop, breaks the ocean’s peace,
your aura blends with the universe’s prana.

The journey has begun,
you’ve taken the path,
impossible to turn back,
it’s useless to remind,
in search of an inner contact,
feeling the power , reconcile with it.

The weight of life burdens your existence,
desire to go beyond,
existential emptiness lies in your soul,
result of your suffering,
spiritual loneliness.

Limited by time and the fragile truth
that the human mind elaborates,
the heart get used
to hypocrisy of love.

Like stars and planets doomed to dance in space,
attracting and rejecting without merging,
soul and body will live an unstable balance
condemned to the full weight of matter,
feeding false illusion of salvation,
human nature’s victims.

Sensing the mirage,
perceives the utopia,
making sure that we could be loved
through false ideas,
for fear of realize not be one.

Filling the needs
through the superfluity,
justifying failures just like a
necessary act.

Looking for our true self
within blank stares,
stifling man-indifference
becoming part of it.

Processes pain through loneliness,
disconnected from human values,
accomplice of your failure,
reconcile with you,
joining your true god,
restoring the inner order, loving yourself.

Upsetting the harmony,
falling into cosmic chaos,
in contact with the Entity.
