Symbiosis of Creation

Symbiosis of Creation

The Universe perceives the fragility
Of humanity when man
Knows the feeling that pervades him.
In memory we transcend fear
But in the soul we find
A glimmer of hope.

Orbital elements will hit
Our primordial instinct.
The awakening will illuminate
Our entire energy with a divine flow.

Illuminate now a constellation
Transpose your mind towards the spirit.
Make the two worlds meet and merge.

Finally the divine covenant is revealed,
The dances for Aion Teleos
Do not stop in creation.
Existence will finally make a step.

The sun, the moon and the stars
All in one matter.
The implosion of the ions!
The lure from the union of the
Earth and the universe has arrived ….
All has become one

Time and space have become more important than life.
Humanity has found emotion and joy
Where we can all find comfort.

But in the end it’s me who carries everybody’s pain
In my heart ….
Until eternal symbiosis unites us!

Metaphysical Essence

Metaphysical Essence

Alienation of desire
paradigm of psyche,
all searching for an omniscient god
who will illuminate the truth of a now equilibrium.

The true way is not the mind
But the heart.
We will suffer and divide
our ego in several spaces
of endless wisdom.


Finally embrace hate
and focus on the world made of separation,
I ask you to open your mind to a cosmic contact
for a new sun studded with karma

Look at the perversion
which surrounds the whole existence
but close your eyes and imagine
your life without hate-reflecting mirrors,
follow your soul in its search.

subconscious has taken over,
evade the earth,
you have reached transcendental meditation
the planets aligned on the horizon
leave out now from human apprehension,
remain in soothing silence
and escape in primordial paradise
