Biography

Coexistence is a Progressive Death Metal band from Siena, Italy.
Since their beginning in 2015 the band consist in Mirko Battaglia Pitinello on guitars and vocals, Leonardo Bellavista on guitars, Christian Luconi on bass and Alessandro Formichi on drums.
Coexistence first got noticed after the release of their first single "Ultimatum" in the late 2016, this set the first live apperience in mid 2017 that will be the beginning of a huge series of live shows in the local scene.

In January 2018 the band reaches a deal with ETN Records for the release of their first EP "Contact with the Entity" set for May 2018, it was highly acclamated by critics and fans and let the band get noticed in the worldwide Death Metal comunity.

The band were picked to support international bands like Virvum and Bloodshot Dawn for their italian gigs and lead them to record the "Demo 2019" back in April, featuring two new songs that will be later include in the first full-lenght.

The Demo was received really well by fans around the world, it was really important for building a solid fan base and the performance to the prestigious Metaldays 2019 festival in Slovenia.

With the help of their friend and manager Tito Vespasiani the band reaches a deal with Transcending Obscurity Records, one of the biggest metal label in Asia that will release their debut album "Collateral Dimension" in October 2020.
