Dec 01

Italian technical death metal band COEXISTENCE sign to Transcending Obscurity

Published in News
Tagged under

We're proud to announce our new album "COLLATERAL DIMENSION"
it will be released in spring 2020 via Transcending Obscurity Records, more info here: https://tometal.com/italian-technical-death-metal-band-coexistence-sign-to-transcending-obscurity/?fbclid=IwAR1L-HJODNDwmb1azgre_hfEFfo51cDAObussa7uCiXllpoQsyuVEI2jBfs

Label owner Kunal Choksi states, “I’m thrilled to announce the signing of Coexistence to our label. It’s impressive how much they have progressed from the previous material that they self-released. I’m sure the fans of the style and even others will have much to revel in when the full length drops in early 2020. I must thank Tito Vespasiani for helping out with this deal and I’m sure this will be one of the most important releases in the genre next year. Coexistence have done everything right in their capacity and we’ll do our best for them of course.”

Coexistence member Leonardo Bellavista adds, “Teaming up with one of the best metal labels around such as Transcending Obscurity is an incredible goal for us and marks a new beginning for the band! The insane artwork made by master Adam Burke set the atmosphere for the record and Stefano Morabito at 16th Cellar Studio helped us build the perfect tone for it. We’re looking forward to release our first album ‘Collateral Dimension’ in early 2020 and share it with you all!”

Artwork by Nightjar art of Adam Burke (Adam Burke)

Guitars, Bass, Vocals recorded at Void Recording Studio (Leonardo Bellavista)
Drums Recordings, Mix & Master at 16th Cellar Studio (Stefano Morabito)

Management & Booking: Tito Vespasiani (This email address is being protected from spambots. You need JavaScript enabled to view it.)
